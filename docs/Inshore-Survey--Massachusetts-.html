1.2 Indicator name

Inshore Survey (Massachusetts)

Includes variable(s): Benthivore Fall Biomass Index - MA, Benthivore Fall Biomass SE - MA, Benthivore Spring Biomass Index - MA, Benthivore Spring Biomass SE - MA, Benthos Fall Biomass Index - MA, Benthos Fall Biomass SE - MA, Benthos Spring Biomass Index - MA, Benthos Spring Biomass SE - MA, Other Fall Biomass Index - MA, Other Fall Biomass SE - MA, Other Spring Biomass Index - MA, Other Spring Biomass SE - MA, Piscivore Fall Biomass Index - MA, Piscivore Fall Biomass SE - MA, Piscivore Spring Biomass Index - MA, Piscivore Spring Biomass SE - MA, Planktivore Fall Biomass Index - MA, Planktivore Fall Biomass SE - MA, Planktivore Spring Biomass Index - MA, Planktivore Spring Biomass SE - MA

1.3 Indicator brief description

Biomass time series for aggregate species groups from the Massachusetts inshore bottom trawl surveys conducted in Massachusetts state waters.

1.4 Indicator visualization

Each survey shows trends by aggregate group.

2.2 Indicator analysis/testing or history of use

2.2.1 What decision or advice processes are the indicators currently used in?

Indicators from this inshore survey are analogous to those produced by the NEFSC trawl survey in the aggregate survey biomass indicator dataset.

2.2.2 What implications of the indicators are currently listed?

The trends in the Massachusetts inshore survey vary compared to the NEFSC survey. This is driven by different species availability to surveys in time and space as well as the surveys sampling different habitats. Nearshore habitats are important to fish and fisheries so we monitor them as as well as trends from the larger EPUs.
